My Learning Agreement

A core challenge from my second sprint
What are my strengths and limitations?

My biggest strength that will help me on my learning journey with Dev Academy is my background in code. Because I have a degree in Computer Science, even thought the concepts are new to me they will be familiar. I also love working together with people and I am great at dividing and delegating tasks, this is going to be very handy when our cohort starts doing projects together.

Sometimes when I am learning a new topic I will move through the information quickly because I think I already know the gist of it, only to find that we have moved from a -> c and I have missed step b! This can effect my deeper understanding of a topic which limits me later. Although I love working in groups I am a bit awkward around new people because I am shy. This limits me because I don't voice my opinions straight away as it takes me time to become comfortable with people.

How will I manage my workload in this programme?

Everyday I set myself a goal that I think will be attainable by the end of the day and I set myself some "stretch" to complete if I have extra time. Not finishing my set tasks in a day makes me feel like I have achieved nothing so I never set my goal as something unobtainable. I will time myself when I start and finish working and currently I am trying to put in 5 hours a day of solid work, not including all the breaks that I take throughout the day.

How will I seek help in a timely way?

MY first port of call will always be Google, especially for Foundations, someone has probably had my problem and solved it before. Secondly I would contact my cohort through the Slack channels. Failing that I would get in touch directly with my facilitator Engie through slack.

What do I expect from the Dev-Academy facilitation team?

Support when needed during business hours, especially through the main channels - slack and email. I imagine they can give a wide range of support from bugs in my code, to needing to adjust my learning journey because of personal problems, to restaurant recommendations.

What times do I need to block out on my schedule?

I have no other commitments currently, but in February I will be starting a Te Reo course from 12th February on Tuesday evenings from 6pm-9pm. I may need to leave Dev Academy slightly earlier on Tuesdays.
